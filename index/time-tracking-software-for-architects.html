FAQs

How can time tracking help stay within project scope and avoid overservicing clients?

Time tracking helps architectural firms stay within project scope by offering real-time visibility into how employees are using their time. Not only that, but automated architects time tracking software also ensures that architects focus on high-priority tasks and deliver them on time. Therefore, by accurately tracking time and enhancing work management, firms can avoid overservicing clients.

Can I assign different hourly rates for clients in engineering time tracking software?

Yes, TimeBee lets you assign different hourly rates for each employee based on the client they are working with. Our time tracking and billing software for architects and engineers has separate tabs for every client, so you can easily manage which users are assigned to individual clients and then apply specific hourly rates per individual. Through this bill and payroll tracker, you can ensure accurate billing and simplify invoicing.

How do I track billable vs. non-billable hours for different clients in my architecture firm?

With TimeBee’s billable tracker, you can easily track how much time your team spends on each client, down to the last active minute. The software automatically logs productive time and filters out idle hours, so you don’t have to manually label tasks as billable or non-billable. This gives you an accurate breakdown of where client hours are going, helping you invoice confidently and understand which projects are truly worth your team’s time.

Can I generate detailed timesheets to support our invoices for hourly projects?

Yes, using a time billing software for architects, you can generate detailed timesheets that back up your hourly invoices. Since these applications automatically calculate total hours based on login and logout times, architectural firms get a clear breakdown of hours worked per team member on a particular project. Moreover, the built-in adjustment option allows organizations to compensate users if needed. As a result, the clients are billed accurately, and invoices are created for hourly projects.

Is GPS tracking available for on-site work or field visits?

Yes, TimeBee’s mobile app offers GPS for on-site work and field visits. Since architects and engineers often work outside the office, TimeBee captures their location when they clock in and clock out to ensure accountability and confirm their presence at the required site.

Is offline time tracking available for architects working in areas with limited internet access?

Offline time tracking is supported in TimeBee project and time tracking software. Its desktop app continues to track time and overall activity even without an active connection. Once the internet is restored, all recorded data, including work hours and activity logs, is automatically synced with the web dashboard. This makes it ideal for architects and engineers working in areas with limited connectivity.

Can managers approve manual time logs before billing?

Yes, managers can review and approve manual time logs before they are added to billing. Architects can submit time entries for untracked work, like meetings or on-site visits, by selecting the time, adding a description, and choosing an approver. Once submitted, the manager can approve or reject the entry. If approved, the time is automatically added to the employee’s total hours in the timesheet software for engineers. This process ensures accurate billing, payroll, and compensation while maintaining full transparency and control.
