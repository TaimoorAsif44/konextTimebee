FAQs

Can I see which apps and websites my employees spend the most time on?

Yes, employee productivity monitoring software lets you view the apps and websites your employees spend the most time on, ranked from highest to lowest based on total tracked hours. Each entry includes the percentage it contributes to the overall logged time. Within the web and app usage feature, you can filter by productive or unproductive categories to focus on tools that drive or hinder performance.

Will tracking still work for hybrid or remote teams across multiple locations?

Productivity monitoring tools are designed to support hybrid and remote teams working from different locations. They include detailed configuration settings such as start time, end time, late margin, early leave margin, and minimum hours to be logged that ensure accurate time tracking. Time zones can also be set individually for each team member, allowing work logs to align with their local hours.

Can I use the work productivity tracker to identify idle time during work hours?

Yes, with TimeBee, you can effectively track idle time during work hours. It monitors keyboard and mouse activity to detect periods of inactivity, which are then recorded as idle time. When no input is detected for a set duration, the system triggers a distraction alert, prompting the employee to resume work. Time logging only continues once the user interacts with the alert, ensuring that unproductive periods are not counted as active work.

How are screenshots used in productivity tracking, and are they secure?

Productivity monitoring software like TimeBee uses screenshots to capture employee screen activity at regular intervals to help employers identify workflow inefficiencies and patterns of unproductivity early on. All these screenshots are securely stored within an end-to-end encrypted dashboard that only enables authorized personnel to access them. Since TimeBee complies with major data protection and employee monitoring laws, including the GDPR, CCPA, etc., employee privacy is protected while maintaining transparency.

How do I differentiate between productive and unproductive activities in workforce productivity software?

Differentiating between productive and unproductive activities involves rating the websites and applications your employees use during work hours. When using advanced software for tracking employee productivity, the system logs all accessed platforms and displays them as unrated on the web dashboard. From there, you can manually assign each one a rating, i.e., productive or unproductive. Over time, as usage patterns become clearer, you can assess how much time is spent on meaningful tasks versus distractions.

How long are employee productivity analytics stored, and can I export historical productivity reports?

Employee performance data is stored for up to a year, allowing employers to access and review past employee performance data whenever needed. These historical productivity reports can be exported easily from the dashboard by choosing from predefined time filters such as daily, weekly, or monthly, or by setting a custom date range. Once the desired period is selected, reports can be downloaded in either PDF or CSV format.

What data should I focus on if I only have 10 minutes to check the productivity tracker for employees?

If you only have 10 minutes to check team productivity, the team dashboard on TimeBee offers a quick yet comprehensive overview. It displays summaries of total productive and unproductive time across the organization. You can also instantly see who the most and least productive employees are, along with those logging the highest and lowest number of work hours.

Will productivity monitoring tools slow down employee computers or affect performance?

While other monitoring tools can cause slowdowns on employee systems by consuming high memory, our team productivity tracker is not the same. It is designed to be lightweight and efficient, which enables it to log activity data with minimal system resource usage. Therefore, unlike some tools that cause noticeable lag, TimeBee ensures smooth device operation and detailed productivity insights without compromising the overall user experience.
