FAQs

What kind of reports can I generate from the data in a time punch app?

You can get detailed reports that help with workforce and project management. These include total hours worked and timesheets with exact start and end times. Moreover, you can view attendance logs plus leave requests, along with payroll summaries. Activity reports further track productivity by analyzing web and app usage. Not only that, but you can also get project- and task-specific time logs for client billing and cost tracking.

What happens if an employee loses internet connection while clocking in?

If an employee loses connection while using the employee check-in app, TimeBee’s offline mode takes over automatically. They can still clock in, and their hours will continue to be tracked in the employee check in app. Once the internet is restored, all data syncs to the web dashboard without any loss.

Can I see the location where employees are clocking in from?

Yes. TimeBee captures the exact GPS location each time an employee clocks in or out with their mobile app. Every log includes a timestamp and location data, allowing managers to monitor attendance in real time. It is especially useful for preventing time theft and managing remote or field-based teams

How do I handle clock-ins for remote or hybrid teams?

Handling clock-ins for a remote or hybrid team is easy with the right time clock software. You do not need to invest in extra hardware that adds cost and complexity. Instead, use TimeBee, which turns any phone or computer into a time clock. Employees can log their hours with a single tap from anywhere, and their time is instantly tracked and synced to the central dashboard, giving managers full visibility in real time.

Can I approve entries on a time punch clock app before they are added to payroll?

Yes, a time clock lets managers approve time entries before they’re added to payroll. Employees can submit manual time requests by entering details like the time range, project name, task description, and the assigned approver. Managers can review each request from the dashboard and choose to approve or reject it based on accuracy or company policy.

Is it possible to get a daily summary of who worked and for how long?

A time clock tool provides daily summaries through automated online attendance sheets. These sheets show each employee’s total hours worked, along with their clock-in and clock-out times. This helps managers track work hours and payroll more accurately, while also making it easier to spot patterns, adjust schedules, and manage teams more effectively.

Should I use a free time clock app for managing employee hours?

Using a free tool might seem cost-effective, but it lacks accurate reports, real-time tracking, approval workflows, and strong data security. Many free apps also have usage limits or ads that can disrupt your workflow. For reliable employee hour management, TimeBee is a better choice. It offers detailed timesheets, attendance reports, productivity insights, project tracking, and a centralized dashboard to make sure every hour is counted.

Is a time clock app for small business suitable for tracking field staff?

Yes, a mobile-friendly time clock is a great fit for tracking field staff. With GPS-enabled clock-ins from any smartphone, it is easy to manage remote or on-site teams in real time. For growing businesses, it is also important that the app scales with you and supports more users, projects, and reporting needs as your operations expand.
