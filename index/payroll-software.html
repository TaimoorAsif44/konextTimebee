FAQs

Can I automatically calculate gross pay based on tracked work hours and hourly rates?

Yes, an advanced payroll tracker app can automatically calculate gross pay using tracked work hours and assigned hourly rates. Once an employee’s time is recorded, the system multiplies the total hours worked by their hourly wage to determine gross earnings. This includes regular and overtime hours, depending on the app's settings.

Can employees see their logged hours and earnings before payroll is finalized?

Yes, TimeBee allows employees to view their logged hours and estimated earnings before payroll is finalized. Doing so enhances transparency and allows employees to confirm their time entries and report issues proactively. If they come across time entries not properly logged, they can send manual time requests to their managers for accurate payroll calculation.

Can I filter payroll data by specific cost centers?

Yes, with Timebee, you can filter payroll data by specific cost centers, like users, departments, clients, and projects, for more precise insights. In the payroll section, you can easily view earnings and tracked time based on specific team members or groups. Additionally, with Timebee’s project cost feature, you can see exactly how much each employee has earned for specific tasks, based on their logged hours and hourly rates.

Can I restrict access so that only managers or HR staff can view pay-related data?

Timebee uses role-based access controls to manage who can view pay-related data. For example, regular users with member access can only see their own payroll information, ensuring transparency while protecting sensitive data. On the other hand, users with the admin role, such as managers or HR staff, have broader access and can view payroll details for all employees. This setup ensures that payroll data remains secure and is only accessible to authorized personnel

How do I handle split shifts, night shifts, or different pay rates in payroll calculation?

TimeBee’s payroll software lets you accurately track payroll hours for various shift types, including split and night shifts. For cases where different pay rates apply, you can set a base rate per user and use the adjustment option to add any additional amounts.

Can I export payroll data to send to my accountant or payroll provider?

Using Timebee, you can easily export payroll data to share with your accountant or payroll provider. The payroll is downloaded in CSV format and includes total hours worked, hourly rates, adjustments, and total pay for each employee in a clear, organized layout. When downloading, you can select all employees or filter data by specific departments. Moreover, you can also choose a specific time range, up to the previous year, for payroll export. This ensures smooth collaboration with external payroll services or accountants without manual data compilation.

How far back can I view payroll history for audits or employee disputes?

Payroll history is accessible for up to one year in a sophisticated and reliable payroll tracking software, enabling thorough audits and resolving employee disputes. This includes detailed records of hours worked, pay rates, and any adjustments made during the selected period. Having access to a full year of payroll data ensures transparency and accuracy, helping managers and HR teams address questions or concerns efficiently.

Can I track payroll costs per project or client for better budgeting?

Yes, you can track payroll costs by both project and client to support more accurate budgeting and profitability analysis. For client-based tracking, TimeBee calculates payroll using both the client billing rate and the internal user rate, helping you easily determine profit margins on outsourced work. For project-level insights, payroll is broken down by individual tasks and users. Each user’s time spent on a project is recorded, and their payroll is calculated accordingly for a clear view of labor costs per project.

Can payroll and timekeeping software track contractor payments separately from employee payroll?

Contractor payroll can be tracked separately by assigning contractors to a dedicated department within the system. Using the group-wise payroll filter, you can view total payouts, rates, and hours worked exclusively for contractors. This separation simplifies audits and maintains a clear separation between different types of labor costs within your organization.

Can I use a free payroll app to calculate my employees’ salaries?

Free payroll apps may seem convenient, but they come with serious risks. These tools typically lack strong security protocols, which puts sensitive employee data at risk of breaches. They also have limited data backup, increasing the chances of information loss during software glitches or updates. Moreover, inaccurate calculations due to missing features or poor integrations can lead to incorrect payouts. However, Timebee eliminates these concerns with secure, reliable payroll data.
