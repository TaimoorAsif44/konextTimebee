FAQs

How can a remote employee monitoring software distinguish between active work and idle time?

With TimeBee, distinguishing between active work and idle time is straightforward. Its remote work time tracking automatically detects keyboard and mouse activity to log active hours, while periods of inactivity are marked as idle. You can customize idle timeout settings to match your workflow. This ensures that only productive hours are recorded and managers get a clear picture of time spent without relying on manual input or constant supervision.

Can a work from home time tracker accurately monitor work hours across different time zones?

Yes, it’s possible to track work hours across different time zones using time tracking software for remote employees. These applications typically record time based on each employee’s local settings and automatically sync the data to a central dashboard. Employees can log their hours consistently, while managers access the information in a unified format, regardless of location. This makes it easier to compare and coordinate work across global teams for fair and transparent tracking.

Can time tracking remote employees show productivity trends and time usage patterns?

Yes, with TimeBee, you can view detailed productivity trends and time usage patterns for each team member. The platform breaks down time spent on productive and unproductive tasks, along with active hours and idle time. Managers can access visual reports that highlight individual work habits, performance patterns, and workload distribution. These insights help optimize task allocation and provide better support for remote teams.

Does remote employee monitoring and time tracking software track split shifts or irregular schedules?

Yes, TimeBee supports split shifts and irregular schedules through flexible configuration options. Its remote worker time tracking feature lets you set custom start and end times for the entire organization or change them for individual team members. This way, you can accurately keep track of hours, even when employees work nontraditional shifts or across varied time blocks.

Can I group offshore employees by team or department and compare performance metrics?

Yes, TimeBee offers built-in grouping and comparison tools to streamline time tracking for remote employees. Managers can organize team members into departments, roles, or project-based groups for clearer visibility. Within these groups, it’s easier to monitor productivity, activity levels, and logged hours in a structured view. You can also compare performance across teams to uncover strengths, address gaps, and guide smarter resource and performance decisions.

How do I track time for remote employees working across multiple projects?

You need an advanced remote teams hour tracker with project-level logging to monitor time across multiple projects. These applications let employees assign hours to specific tasks or projects as they switch between them to ensure accurate records. For example, the TimeBee app allows precise tracking across tasks and records billable and non-billable hours of remote teams, making it easier for managers to monitor productivity, manage workloads, and generate client-ready reports.

Can I control who has access to view, edit, or manage team time data?

Yes, TimeBee provides detailed access controls through defined user roles—Owner, Admin, and Member—to help you manage team time data securely. Owners have all access, while Admins can manage teams, review timesheets, and adjust specific settings. Members, on the other hand, can only track their own time and view personal reports. These role-based permissions ensure that each user accesses only what is relevant to their responsibilities.

What happens if the remote employee’s internet connection is unstable?

If a remote employee’s internet connection is unstable, TimeBee continues tracking time and activity locally on the device without interruption, ensuring no data is lost during offline periods. Once the connection is restored, our remote employee monitoring software syncs all tracked data—including time logs and activity metrics—automatically to the system. This way, managers get an accurate and complete record, even when team members face connectivity issues.

Can I get daily or weekly summaries of what my remote team worked on?

Yes, most time tracking apps provide options to receive daily or weekly summaries of your team’s activity. By recording a timesheet for remote workers, you can view logged hours, task breakdowns, and activity levels over selected time periods. These summaries inform managers about work progress and productivity trends and ensure accountability, especially when overseeing remote teams across different locations.
