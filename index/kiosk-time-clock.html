FAQs

Is the kiosk time clock secure from buddy punching or fake check-ins?

Yes, the kiosk clock is designed to eliminate buddy punching and ensure accurate attendance. It combines PIN-based access, real-time photo verification, and location tracking at the time of clock-in or clock-out to ensure only the actual employee can log attendance. You can set up separate kiosks for each location, which makes it easier to manage employees across different sites. Attendance is only recorded when employees check in at their assigned location, adding another layer of accuracy and minimizing time theft.

What type of device do I need to set up a kiosk clock?

Setting up a kiosk clock app is flexible and simple. You can run the app on any internet-enabled device, such as a tablet, desktop, or mobile phone. There's no need for expensive hardware installations. For example, the TimeBee’s time kiosk app uses a unique URL to convert any device into a kiosk. Simply open the URL, enter the Admin PIN, and the device will switch to kiosk mode. From there, employees can enter their PIN to clock in or out. This universal compatibility makes it easy to deploy the kiosk anywhere – whether in an office, on a job site, or even in a mobile setup.

Does the kiosk time app require an internet connection to work properly?

Yes, a stable internet connection is recommended for the time clock kiosk to function properly. It enables real-time syncing of attendance logs, secure photo verification, GPS tracking, and accurate time records across locations. If you need instant updates for clock-ins and clock-outs, internet access is essential. However, some advanced apps like TimeBee can temporarily store data offline. When the connection is restored, the app automatically syncs all pending records to maintain data accuracy.

Can I use the kiosk time app across multiple job sites?

Absolutely. The kiosk clock-in app is built to provide an opportunity to expand and scale your business without compromising on distance and discipline. You can set up individual kiosks for each location with location-specific settings for precise tracking and monitoring. Each kiosk can be customized with unique access URLs and expiration timers, ensuring both flexibility and security. Whether you're managing offices, warehouses, or remote sites, it provides synchronized data and a centralized dashboard. This allows you to monitor employee activity, verify attendance, and maintain consistent records across every location.

Can I track both salaried and hourly employees through the kiosk tracking app?

Clock in kiosk apps are used to calculate accurate time without missing any time. For hourly staff, it helps track punch-in/punch-out times for accurate payroll and billable hours. For salaried employees, it promotes discipline and performance accountability by monitoring active presence on-site. Additionally, they can also be helpful for hybrid and field workers. For example, TimeBee’s time synchronization feature automatically merges app-based tracking with kiosk punch-ins. So, if an employee works remotely in the morning and checks in later at the office kiosk, their total time is synced and recorded seamlessly.

Does the time tracking kiosk log how long employees stay active at the job site?

Yes, the kiosk time tracker app records the total active hours between clock-in and clock-out. This allows you to track how long each employee is present at a job site. With built-in GPS and photo verification, you also get visual and location-based proof of attendance. The system helps identify missed check-ins or irregular patterns, ensuring accurate payroll and greater accountability. Plus, it gives managers clear insight into workforce activity and makes it easier to plan shifts, track productivity, and allocate resources efficiently.
