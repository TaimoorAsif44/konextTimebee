FAQs

Can I generate reports to see who is consistently logging excessive overtime?

Yes, you can generate reports that highlight employees who consistently log excessive overtime. Advanced overtime tracking tools include built-in reporting features that provide operational insight. These reports surface patterns by individual or team, helping managers identify workload imbalances early. With clear visibility into overtime trends, you can rebalance workloads, protect employee well-being, stay compliant, and make smarter staffing decisions without relying on manual tracking.

Is it possible to distinguish between daily and weekly overtime calculations?

Yes, it is entirely possible to distinguish between daily and weekly overtime calculations. Daily overtime is based on hours worked beyond a set limit in a single day, while weekly overtime is calculated on total hours exceeding the standard threshold for a workweek. Organizations that use advanced software to track overtime hours can handle both automatically. These applications automatically apply the correct rules to ensure compliance, reduce manual errors, and streamline payroll processes with greater accuracy and efficiency.

Is there a way to approve or reject logged overtime before it goes to payroll?

Absolutely. TimeBee includes built-in approval workflows that let managers review and verify each overtime entry before it moves to payroll. This ensures accuracy and helps enforce internal policies for better control over labor costs. By validating entries in advance, you can strengthen compliance and create a more transparent, accountable approval process across the organization.

Is there a mobile option for monitoring overtime when employees work off-site?

Yes, the TimeBee app offers a mobile app that allows employees to log hours from anywhere. This gives managers real-time visibility into off-site work without delays or manual check-ins. Supervisors can review entries remotely and maintain oversight even with distributed teams. With mobile access in place, organizations can ensure accurate overtime tracking and consistent record-keeping, regardless of location.

How do I track overtime for part-time or contract workers?

You need a customized setup to track overtime for part-time or contract workers. Start by setting role-specific thresholds based on contracts or applicable labor laws. Use an employee overtime hours tracker that supports custom rules for different worker types. This ensures accurate calculations, reduces compliance risks, and eliminates manual errors.

How do I differentiate between paid and unpaid overtime hours in reporting?

To differentiate between paid and unpaid overtime hours in reports, configure your tool with clear overtime categories. Define rules that separate compensable hours from non-compensable ones, like volunteer time or unapproved overtime. With TimeBee, you can apply custom tags and approval workflows, making it easy to filter reports by payment status. This improves payroll accuracy and helps organizations manage labor more effectively.
