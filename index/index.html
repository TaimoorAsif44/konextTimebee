FAQs

Can I generate automated payroll reports based on my academic team’s hours worked?

Yes, with TimeBee, generating payroll reports is simple and efficient. Our employee monitoring app automatically calculates payroll based on the hours worked by your team members. Just input each staff member’s hourly rate, and TimeBee will track their time and compute their total pay accordingly. You can also make manual adjustments to account for overtime, bonuses, or other custom payments for accurate payrolls.

Can I see how my academic staff is spending their work hours?

Using TimeBee, you can gain detailed insights into how your academic staff is spending their work hours. Since the platform allows team members to log time against specific tasks and projects, you get a clear breakdown of their daily activities. With these logs at hand, you can identify which tasks or projects are consuming most of your team’s hours and how to better distribute the workload. Moreover, you can improve the staff’s productivity by addressing workflow disruptions, wasted hours, and areas of improvement.

Can teachers clock in and out using their mobile devices?

Many assume that employee monitoring tools are limited to desktop use, but that’s not the case with more advanced solutions like TimeBee. Our time tracking software for educational staff allows teachers to easily clock in and out using their mobile devices. This mobile functionality not only offers time-logging flexibility for educators on the go but also helps administrators monitor their location every time they clock in or out.

How do we track overtime for administrative staff in schools?

Administrative staff in educational institutions often work beyond standard hours due to the nature of their responsibilities. However, manually tracking this overtime can be time-consuming, which potentially increases workload and operational costs. In its place, TimeBee, a time tracking application, offers a better solution by automatically recording each employee’s work hours, including start and end times, along with a detailed daily activity timeline. Therefore, if staff members clock out later than their scheduled time, it can be flagged as potential overtime. These extra hours can then be reviewed and adjusted in the payroll within the app for accurate compensation.

Is TimeBee compatible with both Windows and Mac systems?

Yes, TimeBee is fully compatible with both Windows and Mac operating systems. Whether your team is using desktop computers or laptops, the software runs smoothly across both platforms. This cross-platform compatibility ensures that all school staff members, regardless of their device preference, can access TimeBee’s full range of features without any limitations.

Can we track remote or hybrid educational staff working from home?

Yes, you can. As online education becomes increasingly common, it is important to have clear visibility into how staff are managing their responsibilities outside the physical classroom. Tracking remote or hybrid educational staff is easy with advanced employee monitoring applications like TimeBee. The app makes this possible by accurately logging work hours and capturing key activities such as class schedule, work distribution, engagement of students, and overall device usage during work time. This ensures that educators remain accountable and productive, even when working from home.

What kind of reports can an educational institution’s administrative block generate from time-tracking data?

An educational institution’s administrative block can use TimeBee to generate reports on staff attendance, work hours, overtime, performance, leave patterns, etc. These insights, therefore, help track punctuality, identify idle time, and ensure policy compliance. The time-tracking data also supports payroll accuracy and budget planning through overtime and cost analysis. Lastly, a dashboard overview like the total team's hours, top projects, most productive/unproductive users, highest/lowest work hours, etc., enables the administration to make better decisions and manage resources effectively.

How is educational workers' activity data stored, and who has access to it?

TimeBee securely stores educators’ activity data using features such as screenshots, camera captures, activity summaries, and web and app usage tracking. All data is collected directly from the user’s desktop and automatically uploaded to the TimeBee web dashboard. However, access to this data is strictly controlled to ensure privacy and security. Only users assigned the “Admin” role can view the full activity logs and reports.

Can different university or school campuses, or departments, be managed under one account?

Yes, TimeBee is a time tracking software for universities & schools that operates at an organizational level. Designed as an enterprise solution, it efficiently supports large user bases, making it ideal for managing multiple campuses or departments under a single account. To simplify oversight, the app also allows you to create groups, where staff can be categorized together based on their campuses or departments. Therefore, using this categorization, administrators can easily monitor activity data collectively if needed.

How does TimeBee handle different pay rates for different activities (teaching, meetings, after-school programs)?

Since TimeBee assigns pay rates at the organizational level, each staff member has a fixed hourly rate regardless of the specific activity they perform. However, to account for variations in compensation, such as additional pay for extra duties or extended hours, administrators can use the “Adjustment” feature. This allows schools and universities to add overtime, bonuses, or other activity-based payments directly to the payroll, ensuring accurate and fair compensation for all educational staff.
